Frequently asked questions:

Q: Is this compatible with Android?
A: This website only provides an iPhone version, but there are Snapchat hacks for Android available on Android.

Q: Will this replace the regular Snapchat app?
A: No, Snapchat++ will be installed alongside the normal Snapchat app from the App Store.

Q: What features does this mod add?
A: It adds screenshot suppression (so no-one will be notified if you screenshot their snap), ghost mode (so you can see snaps without marking them as read), and a lot more features.

Q: Help! It's not installing/opening and says the integrity couldn't be verified!
A: That means the certificate I use got revoked by Apple. If you want to report a revoke, please email me at support@bomberfish.ca so I can get it up and running again.

Q: Will my Snapchat account get banned?
A: As with all modded apps, there is no guarantee that you will be safe from a ban. However, these bans are exceedingly rare.

Q: Help! It doesn't open and says it needs to verify the app!
A: Apple most likely blacklisted your device from the certificate. You can either wait for it to be resigned after a revoke or you can factory reset your device, which usually works.
